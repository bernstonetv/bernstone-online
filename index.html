🎣 Fishing Game Commands

🎣 FISHING GAME COMMANDS
Goal: Fish, earn BernCoins, level up, and complete your fishing collection!

🏆 Fishing & Progression
!fish → Catch a fish! Earn points, BernCoins, and XP. Cooldown: 1 min.
> Higher levels catch more fish per cast (max 5 at Lvl 100+).
> Rarer fish = more rewards.

!fishlevel / !flvl
> Shows level, XP, total fish caught, biggest fish, and earned titles.

!fishcheck / !fc
> View your caught fish vs total available.

!fishtimeleft / !ftl
> Shows how much time is left in the fishing event.

!nexttitle / !nt
> How far you are from your next title rank.

!uniques
> List of all remaining Uncaught Unique Fish.

🎣 Bait & Boosts
!buybait / !bb
> Spend 0.1% of total points for 10 baited casts.
> Baits increase rare fish chance. Can stack.

!givebait / !gib (user)
> Send bait to another user (costs 0.1% of your points).

!remainingbait / !rb
> Check how many baited casts you have left.

💰 Currency & Leaderboards
!berncoins / !bc
> View your BernCoins balance (during events only).

!topfish / !tf
> Top 10 by level.

!biggestfish / !bf
> Largest fish caught (by weight, first caught wins tie).

!topfishcaught / !tfc
> Ranked by number of fish caught.

!topfishweight / !tfw
> Ranked by total weight of fish caught.

!topbc / !tbc
> Top 10 by BernCoin holdings.

🧺 Net Casting
!castnet / !cn
> Cast a net to catch 5–15 fish. Cooldown: 15 min.
> XP only — no BernCoins. Can be boosted by bait.
> Only usable during fishing events.

🏹 Bounty System
!bounty
> Check the current bounty fish.
> Catch it for 50× XP and BernCoin bonus (manual fishing only).
> A new bounty spawns when the last one is caught.

📜 Special Achievements & Claims  
!claimrare → Claim a 1 Streamloots Pack for catching all Rare fish.  
!claimepic → Claim 2 Streamloots Packs for catching all Epic fish.  
!claimlegendary → Claim 3 Streamloots Packs for catching all Legendary fish.  
!claimmythic → Claim 4 Streamloots Packs for catching all Mythic fish.  
!claimlvl → Claim 1 Streamloots Pack for every 25 levels.  
(Level 25 = 1 Pack, Level 50 = 2 Packs, Level 75 = 3 Packs, etc.)

🌊 Currents System  
The sea is alive, and it changes as you fish! During your journey, you'll encounter dynamic Currents — temporary sea events that can enhance your fishing experience. Currents effects only apply to !fish.

These events happen automatically every 30 minutes, or they can be manually summoned by Streamloots Cards.

Use !currents to check which events are currently active!

⚡ Types of Sea Currents:
🌩 Stormy Waters  
> Common and Uncommon fish are easier to catch.

🌕 Full Moon  
> Greatly increases the chance of catching Mythic fish.

🧠 Double XP  
> Earn 2× XP for every fish you catch.

🐟 School of Fish  
> Catch up to 3× more fish per cast!

💰 Golden Tide  
> All BernCoins earned are doubled during this current.

🔴 Red Moon  
> Increases your chances of catching a Unique fish — a rare and permanent trophy from the depths!

💨 Rushing Current  
> Cooldowns for !fish and !castnet are halved.  
> Stacks with gear for turbo fishing!

🌪️ Cyclone Eye  
> Chaos on the sea! XP and BernCoins become wildly unpredictable (0.0× to 2.0× multiplier).

🪙 Bounty Frenzy  
> Catching the Bounty Fish during this current grants massive 100× XP and BernCoin rewards!

⏳ Duration:  
> Automatically triggered events last 5 minutes.  
> Manually summoned currents via Streamloots Cards last 30 minutes and extend if already active.

🪝 Rods & Gear

!buygear / !bg  
→ Spend 0.25 BernCoins to reduce your !fish cooldown from 60s to 20s & !castnet cooldown from 15m to 5m for 5.5 minutes.  
Can be used again to extend duration.  
Use !geartime / !gt to check remaining time.

!buyrod <rarity> / !br  
→ Boost your chance of catching a specific fish rarity for your next 10 !fish casts (not !castnet).  
Use !rodcheck / !rc to check your rod and remaining casts.  
Buying the same rod again adds 10 more uses.  
You can only have 1 rod active at a time.

🎣 Rod Costs (BernCoins):  
Rare Rod → 0.10  
Epic Rod → 0.20  
Legendary Rod → 0.35  
Mythic Rod → 0.50

🎯 Rod Effect:  
Increases the chance of catching that rarity.  
Boosted rarity weight = 3×

💱 BernCoin Conversion  
!cbc <amount> or !cbc all  
→ Convert BernCoins into points at a rate of:  
1 BernCoin = 1% of total system points  
Example: !cbc 0.5 or !cbc all  
⏳ 60-minute cooldown between conversions

🧱 Milestone Rewards  
→ Earn BernCoins by catching enough fish of each rarity — rewards scale infinitely!

🎯 Claiming  
Use !claimmilestone <rarity> or /cm <rarity> to claim your milestone reward.  
Every claim increases the fish required and the reward.  
Milestones are based on lifetime catches (they don’t reset).

📊 Progress  
Use !milestoneprogress or /mp to check your current milestone progress across all rarities.  
See how close you are to your next reward.

📈 Examples  
Common:  
Catch 1,000 → +5 BC  
Then 2,000 → +6.25 BC  
Then 4,000 → +7.81 BC  
(keeps scaling...)

Mythic:  
Catch 25 → +30 BC  
Then 50 → +37.5 BC  
Then 100 → +46.88 BC  
(infinite scaling!)

🧠 Notes  
You can claim for every rarity: common → mythic  
Each milestone gets progressively harder and more rewarding  
BernCoins are added directly to your balance

🛥️ Boat System  
→ Automatically fish every few minutes during a fishing event — perfect for AFK or lurking!

💰 Buying a Boat  
Use !buyboat mythic, legendary, or epic  
Each boat costs:  
Mythic → 3 BernCoins  
Legendary → 1.0 BernCoin  
Epic → 0.4 BernCoins  
Boat runs for 1.5 hours per purchase  
Buying the same tier again extends the time  
Only one boat active at a time — wait for expiration to change tiers

🕒 How It Works  
Boats auto-cast based on tier:  
Mythic → every 1 minute  
Legendary → every 3 minutes  
Epic → every 5 minutes  
Earn XP and BernCoins on every cast  
Fully automatic once launched — no input needed

🌊 Boats Are Affected By:  
✔️ Double XP → More XP per catch  
✔️ School of Fish → Multiplies fish count  
✔️ Full Moon → Rare chance for Mythic fish  
✔️ Stormy Waters → Boosts Common & Uncommon fish  
✔️ Cyclone → Makes XP and BernCoins wild and unpredictable

🚫 Boats Are NOT Affected By:  
❌ Red Moon → No unique fish from boats  
❌ Rushing Current → Boats do not fish faster  
❌ Golden Tide → No BernCoin boost  
❌ Bounty system → Only manual fishing can hit bounty rewards

🛠️ Extra Notes  
Boats do not use bait or rods  
No unique fish can be caught by boats  
Boat expires automatically after 1.5 hours (or after time extension)  
Use !boattime or !bt to check how much time your boat has left

🎯 Hidden Reel Fish  
!reel (number) → Guess the hidden number (1–30) to catch a mysterious fish.

🕵️ How it works:  
• A fish with a secret number spawns every 15–30 minutes.  
• Anyone can guess the number.  
• First correct guess catches it.  
• Cooldown: 15s per person.  
• Fish despawns after 5 minutes if not caught.  
• Rewards: 100× XP + 10× BernCoins based on fish rarity.  
• ✨ Rarer fish give higher rewards.  
• 🐟 Anyone watching can participate!

⛏️ Mining Game Commands

💎 Gem Mining Game Guide
Welcome to the Gem Mining Game! Collect stones, create powerful gems, and climb the leaderboard! 🪨✨

🪓 Basic Commands
!mine
> Mine a random stone (1-minute cooldown).
> Can be reduced to 30s with gloves.
> Max bag size: 300 stones (upgradeable to 500).

!dynamyte
> Blast up to 20 random fragments instantly (5-minute cooldown).
> Costs 0.3% of total points.

!bag / !frags
> Shows how many and which stones are in your bag.

!synth
> Combine 8–12 stones into a gem with a procedurally generated name and purity.
> The more similar stones used = higher purity!

💎 Gem Management
!gems
> View your entire gem inventory.

!inspect 
> Shows stones used to create your gem.
> Example: !inspect Crystalite
> You can inspect others too: !inspect @user Crystalite

!dismantle 
> Break a gem down and reclaim some of its original stones.

!sellgem  or !sg 
> Sell your gem for points.
> Payout is exponential based on purity.
> 100% purity = 10% of total points in the system.

🔁 Fusion & Refining
!fusegem   or !fg
> Combine 2 gems into a new fused gem.
> Purity averages with bonus for overlapping stones.
> Only similar gems will create strong results.
> Cooldown: 60s

!refine  
> Try to increase a gem’s purity using a frag.
> Matching stone = higher success chance.
> Success: +0.1% to +1.5%
> Failure: -0.1% to -1%
> Cooldown: 60s

!editgem 
> Randomly modifies a gem’s fragments.
> Actions: add, remove, or replace a fragment.
> Uses frags from your bag (add/replace).
> Costs 0.05% of total points.
> Cooldown: 15s

🎓 Stone Specialization
!specs or !specs @user
> See your frag mastery levels.
> Synthesizing with a stone levels it up.
> Higher levels = more purity when used.

⛏️ Gear & Upgrades
!buyaxe
🪓 Get 10 boosted mines (2–5 stones each).
> Cost: 0.1% of total system points.
> Re-buying adds +10 more charges.

!buyglove / !buygloves
🧤 Reduces !mine cooldown to 30s for 5 minutes.
> Re-buying extends the timer.
> Cost: 0.1% of total points.

!buybag
> Expands fragment bag by +10 slots.
> Cost: 0.5% of total points.

!buygembag
> Expands gem storage by +1 slot.
> Cost: 0.5% of total points.

!buysynth
> Reduces !synth cooldown to 30 seconds for 5 minutes.
> Cost: 0.1% of total system points.

!buyref / !buyrefiner
> Reduces !refine cooldown to 30 seconds for 5 minutes.
> Cost: 0.1% of total system points.

!minebuff
> Shows your pickaxe uses, glove time remaining, and miner status.

🤖 Auto Miners
Buy automated miners that generate stones passively every 60s for 1 hour!

!buyminer1 / !buyminer3 / !buyminer5 / !buyminer7 / !buyminer9
> Auto-mines 1–9 stones per tick.
> You cannot buy a new tier if one is active.
> Buying same tier again extends the timer.
> Cost ranges from 0.2% – 1.0% of total points.

🏆 Leaderboards
!topgems, !topsynth, !toprefine, !topfuse, !ta , !toptraders

🧠 Strategy Tips
• Repeating stones in !synth increases purity.
• Level up your stone mastery for long-term purity boosts.
• Use !inspect to pick optimal refining stones.
• Fusing similar gems = stronger results.
• Miners + pickaxe + gloves = max idle gain.
• Only 1 miner can be active at a time — extend it, don’t replace!

✨ Gem Infusion
!usegem / !ug 
> Consume high-purity gem for XP.
> XP = purity × 1.5 to each frag used. CD: 5m.

⚡ Gem Charging
!chargegem / !cg 
> Uses 100+ frags to try to boost purity.
> Match = +3.3/gem size %. Fail = gem destroyed. CD: 1m
