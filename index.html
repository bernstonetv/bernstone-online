BernStone Online

🎣 Fishing Game Commands

Goal: Fish, earn BernCoins, level up, and complete your fishing collection!

🏆 Fishing & Progression
!fish – Catch a fish! Cooldown: 1 min.
!fishlevel / !flvl – Your level, XP, biggest catch, titles.
!fishcheck / !fc – Collection progress.
!fishtimeleft / !ftl – Event time remaining.
!nexttitle / !nt – Progress to next title.
!uniques – Uncaught Unique Fish list.

🎣 Bait & Boosts
!buybait / !bb – Buy baits (0.1% points). Boosts next 10 !fish.
!givebait / !gib – Gift bait to another user.
!remainingbait / !rb – Check active bait uses.

💰 Currency & Leaderboard
!berncoins / !bc – BernCoins balance.
!topfish / !tf – Top 10 by level.
!biggestfish / !bf – Top 10 biggest fish.
!topfishcaught / !tfc – Most fish caught.
!topfishweight / !tfw – Total weight caught.
!topbc / !tbc – Top BernCoin holders.

🧺 Net Casting
!castnet / !cn – 5–15 fish instantly. (15 min cd)

🏹 Bounty System
!bounty – Catch the target fish for 50x XP/Coins.

📜 Special Achievements
!claimrare → All Rare fish = 1 pack.
!claimepic → All Epic fish = 2 packs.
!claimlegendary → All Legendary = 3 packs.
!claimmythic → All Mythic = 4 packs.
!claimlvl → 1 pack every 25 levels.

🏅 Titles System – Check with !flvl
🎣 Angler Titles – Based on fish caught
⚖️ Hauler Titles – Based on weight
🧠 Collector Titles – Based on rarity sets

🌊 Currents System
Use !currents to check active effects.
Stormy Waters – Easier commons
Full Moon – Higher Mythic chance
Double XP – 2× XP
School of Fish – 3× fish
Golden Tide – 2× Coins
Red Moon – More Uniques
Rushing Current – Halved cooldowns
Cyclone Eye – Randomized rewards
Bounty Frenzy – 100x bounty rewards

🪝 Rods & Gear
!buygear / !bg – Reduce !fish cooldown (to 20s)
!buyrod  – Boost catch chance (10 casts)
!rodcheck / !rc – View rod status

💱 BernCoin Conversion
!cbc  – Convert BC into points (1 BC = 1%)

🧱 Milestone Rewards
!claimmilestone  – Scale-up BernCoins by catch count.
!milestoneprogress – View milestone progress.

🛥️ Boat System
!buyboat  – Auto-fish while AFK
Mythic = every 1m, Legendary = 3m, Epic = 5m
Use !boattime / !bt to view time remaining

🎯 Hidden Reel Fish
!reel <1-30> – Guess the number to catch it. (15s cd)
100x XP + 10x Coins if guessed correctly.

⛏️ Mining Game Commands

Collect stones, synth gems, climb leaderboards!

🪓 Basic Commands
!mine – Get stones. (1m cd, 30s w/ gloves)
!dynamyte – Blast 20 frags. (5m cd)
!bag / !frags – Check stone inventory
!synth – Combine 8–12 stones into a gem

💎 Gem Management
!gems – View all your gems
!inspect  – Show gem composition
!dismantle  – Break gem for stones
!sellgem / !sg  – Sell gem (up to 10% of total pts)

🔁 Fusion & Refining
!fusegem / !fg – Merge 2 gems (1m cd)
!refine   – Boost purity (60s cd)
!editgem  – Randomly change frag makeup (15s cd)

🎓 Specialization
!specs – Check frag mastery (XP = better purity)

⛏️ Gear & Upgrades
!buyaxe – +10 boosted mines
!buyglove – Halve mine cd to 30s
!buybag – +10 frag slots
!buygembag – +1 gem slot
!buysynth – Reduce !synth cd to 30s
!buyref – Reduce !refine cd to 30s
!minebuff – Check all gear/boosts

🤖 Auto Miners
!buyminer1 / 3 / 5 / 7 / 9 – Auto-miners
Generate stones passively for 1 hour
Each tier = more stones/min

🏆 Leaderboards
!topgems – Highest purity gems
!topsynth – Best synths
!toprefine – Top refiners
!topfuse – Best fusions
!ta  – Top 10 by title
!toptraders / !tt – Most gem sales

🧠 Tips
• Repeating stones = more purity
• Master stones with !synth
• Fusing similar gems is powerful
• Use !inspect before refining
• Miners + gloves = idle efficiency

✨ New: Gem Infusion (!usegem / !ug)
Sacrifice 80%+ purity gem → Boost XP of used frags
XP = purity × 1.5 — 5 min cd

⚡ New: Gem Charging (!chargegem / !cg)
Use 100+ frags to boost gem purity
Matching frags = +3.3% purity (chance to destroy gem!)
